Rocket: Cultural Food

Rocket is native to the Mediterranean and Asia. Its wide availability and sharp flavor make it widely popular as a leaf vegetable.

Cultivation

Grown as an edible and popular herb in Italy since Roman times.

Culinary

Since Roman times in Italy, raw rocket has been added to salads. It often is added as a garnish to a pizza at the end of or just after baking.

In Apulia, in southern Italy, rocket is cooked to make the pasta dish "cavatiéddi", "in which large amounts of coarsely chopped rocket are added to pasta seasoned with a homemade reduced tomato sauce and pecorino".
